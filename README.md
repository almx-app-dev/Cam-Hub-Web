# Cam-Hub

**Direct camera viewing without another vendor cloud account.**

Cam-Hub connects compatible cameras and recording systems in one consistent interface. Add equipment you own or are authorized to manage, organize its channels, and create a LiveView Mix dashboard across different devices.

[Website](https://almx-app-dev.github.io/Cam-Hub-Web/) · [Features](https://almx-app-dev.github.io/Cam-Hub-Web/product.html) · [Support](https://almx-app-dev.github.io/Cam-Hub-Web/support.html) · [Privacy](https://almx-app-dev.github.io/Cam-Hub-Web/privacy.html) · [Terms](https://almx-app-dev.github.io/Cam-Hub-Web/terms.html)

## Features

- Connect compatible cameras, NVRs, DVRs, and NAS surveillance systems.
- Use ONVIF and RTSP, with additional compatible connections for selected equipment.
- Combine channels from different equipment in a customizable LiveView Mix dashboard.
- Choose from 17 full-screen layouts, including uniform grids and focus layouts.
- View live video, capture snapshots, and search or play recordings when supported by the equipment and account permissions.
- Configure stream quality, image fill mode, watermarks, labels, clocks, images, and HTTP webhook controls.
- Back up supported configuration to your private iCloud using six-digit PIN encryption, or transfer equipment with an encrypted code.

## Privacy by design

Cam-Hub does not require a Cam-Hub account. Equipment credentials are stored in Apple Keychain, and camera connections are made using the equipment and network settings you provide. Optional iCloud backups are encrypted with your PIN before being saved to your private iCloud storage.

See the [Privacy Policy](https://almx-app-dev.github.io/Cam-Hub-Web/privacy.html) for details.

## Compatibility

Cam-Hub supports ONVIF and RTSP-based viewing and has been tested with selected Hikvision, Dahua, and Synology equipment or systems. Actual compatibility varies by model, firmware, protocol implementation, stream format, network configuration, and the permissions granted to the equipment account.

Live streaming, snapshots, main or sub-stream selection, recording search, event classification, timeline seeking, and playback are available only when reported and permitted by the connected camera or recorder.
